Bhagavat Gita

Chapter 1

Sloka 1

Samskrit:

Xarma-kRewre kuru-kRewre samavewA yuyuwsavaH |
mAmakAH pANdavAH ca eva kim akurvawa saFjaya ||

Telugu:

saMjaya! XarmakRewtramaMxu kurukRewramaMxu kUdiyunna yuxXamu ceyu Asakwi kalavAru.
ayina nA vAllu mariyu pANdavulu emi cesAru

Sloka 2

Samskrit:

xqRtvA wu pANdavAnIkaM vyUDaM xuryoXanaswaxA |
AcAryamupasafgamya rAjA vacanamabravIw ||

Telugu:

vyUDamaMxu unna pANdavasEnyamunu cUsina waruvAwa xuryoXanudu appudu AcAryudini samIpiMci rAju I viXamuga mAtlAdAdu.

Sloka 3

Samskrit:

paSyEwAM pANdupuwrAnAmAcArya mahawIM camUM |
vyUDAM xrupaxapuwreNa wava SiRyeNa XImawA ||

Telugu:

AcArya ! nI SiRyudEna XImaMwudEna xrupaxapuwrudi cewa ceyabadda vyUDamaMxu unna pANdupuwrulayokka goppa sEnyamuni cUdu.

Sloka 4

Samskrit:

awra SUrAH mahA-ISu-AsAH BIma-arjuna-samAH yuXi |
yuyuXAnaH virAtaH ca xrupaxaH ca mahAraWaH ||

Telugu:

ikkada BImArjunulaku samAnulEna SUrulu goppa vilukArulu yuyuXAnudu mariyu virAtudu mariyu mahAraWudEna xrupaxudu (unnAru).

Sloka 5

Samskrit:

XqRtakewuH cekiwAnaH kASirAjaH ca vIryavAn |
purujiw kunwiBojaH ca SEbyaH ca narapufgavaH ||

Telugu:

vIrulEna XqRtakewudu cekiwAnudu mariyu kASiraju purujiwwudu kunwiBojudu mariyu narapufgavudEna sEbyudu unnAru.

Sloka 6

Samskrit:

yuXAmanyuH ca vikrAnwaH uwwamOjAH ca vIryavAn |
sOBaxraH xrOpaxeyAH ca sarve eva mahAraWAH ||

Telugu:

vIrudEna yuXAmanyudu mariyu vikrAMwudEna uwwamOjudu mariyu sOBaxrudu mariyu drOpaxeyulu aMxaru niScayamuga mahAraWulu.

Sloka 7

Samskrit:

asmAkaM wu viSiRtA ye wAnniboXa xvija-uwwama |
nAyakAH mama sEnyasya saMjFArWam wAn bravImi we ||

xvijowwma ! nA sEnyaM yokka nAyakulu viSiRtulu ayina mana vAllani weVlusukoV. nIku weliyatAniki ceVpwAnu.

Sloka 8

Samskrit:

BavAn BIRmaH ca karNaH ca kqpaH ca samiwiMjayaH |
aSvawWAmA vikarNaH ca sOmaxawwiH waWA eva ca ||

Telugu:

A prakAramuga mIru BIRmudu mariyu karNudu mariyu kqpAcAryudu mariyu samiwiMjayudu aSvawWAmudu vikarNudu mariyu somaxawwudu niScayamuga (vIrulu unnAru).

Sloka 9

Samskrit:

anye ca bahavaH SUrAH maxarWe wyakwa-jIviwAH |
nAnA-Saswra-praharaNAH sarve yuxXa-viSAraxAH ||

Telugu:

iwarulu mariyu cAlAmaMxi aneka SaswrAlanu XariMcina SUrulu nAkoraku jIviwAlu vaxiliveyatAniki (sixXaMgA unnAru).

Sloka 10

Samskrit:

a-paryApwaM waw asmAkaM balaM BIRmABirakRiwam |
paryApwaM wu ixam eweRAM balaM BImABirakRiwam ||

Telugu:

BIRmABirakRiwamEna mana A balamu aparyApwamu. BImABIrakRiwamEna vIlYlYayokka balamu paryApwamu.

Samskrit:

ayaneRu ca sarveRu yaWA-BAgam avasWiwAH
